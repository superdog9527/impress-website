Your passwords, one touch away

A hardware password manager with fingerprint authentication. Securely send passwords to any device via Bluetooth.

Configure Device View on GitHub

Features

🔒

Fingerprint Security

Your passwords are protected by biometric authentication. Only your fingerprint can unlock and send stored sequences.

📡

Bluetooth HID

Works as a standard Bluetooth keyboard. Compatible with any device that supports Bluetooth - no drivers needed.

📱

Multi-Device

Connect to up to 3 devices simultaneously. Switch between your computer, tablet, and phone with a button press.

⚡

Ultra Low Power

Deep sleep mode consumes only 5μA. Months of battery life with typical usage.

🌐

Web Configuration

Configure your device directly from your browser using Web Bluetooth. No app installation required.

🛠

Open Source

Fully open source hardware and firmware. Audit the code, modify it, or build your own.

How it works

1

Register

Enroll your fingerprints and assign passwords to each one via web config.

2

Connect

Pair with your devices via Bluetooth. Supports up to 3 devices.

3

Touch

Place your finger to authenticate and instantly send your password.
